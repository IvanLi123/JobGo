      Software Development Engineer In Test Interview

      Anonymous Interview Candidate in Seattle, WA (US)
      No Offer
      Positive Experience
      Average Interview

      Application

      I applied through college or university. The process took 4+ weeks. I interviewed at Amazon (Seattle, WA (US)) in April-2014.

      Interview

      Got contacted by HR after the job fair, telling me to finish Online Test. After the test, about 2 weeks, I was told that they want to invite me to go to Seattle for the onsite interview.

      On the onsite day, I talked with four 3 normal interviewers and 1 bar raiser. All the questions are based on CC book, except for the one given by the bar raiser. I did not do well in the second round and third round, which make me fail this opportunity. Due to the NDA, I cannot tell the problem. But, They were not hard. I had 4 problems: one is to solve dependency sequence, one is to solve logic problem (given a function which this to implement another function), one is to solve Tree maximum path problem, and one is to solve anagram problem.

    3. 16 Dec, 2016

      Helpful (3)  

      Software Development Engineer In Test Interview

      Anonymous Interview Candidate in Seattle, WA (US)
      No Offer
      Negative Experience
      Average Interview

      Application

      I applied through an employee referral. The process took 4+ weeks. I interviewed at Amazon (Seattle, WA (US)) in December-2016.

      Interview

      I was referred by a friend and got a mail from them in two weeks time. From the start, the recruiting team was poor in followups. They rescheduled twice and didn't inform me properly. Only after I repeatedly sent mail they gave me an answer. So here is the step by step process:
      1. Recruiter call - Asking about my background and experience
      2. Tech Interview - Few situation based questions and technical questions (be prepared to answer time and space complexity)
      3. Onsite Interview (4 interviews in one day) - interviewers were amazing! question difficulty ranged from medium to hard. Most questions were on graphs. My advice is to solve all problems from leetcode. Very enlightening experience.

      No Offer

      Average Interview

      Application

      I applied through a recruiter. The process took a week. I interviewed at Amazon (Seattle, WA (US)) in March-2016.

      Interview

      Technical phone screen and then a day of full interview loop (4 interviews + lunch interview) from 9:30 am till 2 pm.
      The interviewers were very friendly, answered all my questions.
      Every interview required writing code on a whiteboard.
      To prepare for the interview, the candidate should review the Amazon leadership principles and prepare the personal anecdotes.
      They are asking a lot of situation-based questions. You need to provide specific examples of leadership in you career.

      Interview Questions
